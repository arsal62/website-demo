Paper 2 Exam Tips

Close Reading

Read passages multiple times, first for general understanding and then for specific details. Underline key points and make margin notes to improve comprehension.

Summary Writing

Identify and extract only the relevant points, use your own words where possible, and connect ideas logically. Stick to the word limit precisely.

Vocabulary In Context

Consider the entire sentence and surrounding context when determining word meanings. Choose synonyms that match both meaning and grammatical function.

Time Management

Allocate 15 minutes for reading passages, 50 minutes for comprehension questions, and 25 minutes for summary writing. Leave time to proofread your work.
